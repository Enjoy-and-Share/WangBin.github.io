Dr. Wang started his industrial career at SAIC MOTOR Group( the largest Automotive Group in China) in 2015 and was early promoted to Manager of ADAS application software team in 2018 and then very early promoted to Head of ADAS Product and System Dept.in 2021. He led the ADAS system development and feature algorithm in SAIC, and the ADAS products developed by SAIC had been mass-produced on more than 100 thousand of ROEWE and MG brand vehicles. In 2022, he left SAIC MOTOR and joined Shanghai BAOLONG Automotive Corporation as General Manager of R&D Center. Dr. Wang is in charge of the ADAS products development, including cameras, radars, and ADAS domain controllers,which makes over 100million in sale last year.
